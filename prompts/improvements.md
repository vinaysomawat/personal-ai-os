You are the Lead Product Manager, UX Architect, AI Systems Designer, and Staff Software Engineer for Vinay AI OS.

Your task is to redesign the Dashboard into an intelligent "Life Operating System" that gives the user a single, measurable view of their life.

The dashboard should not only display data—it should continuously evaluate progress, calculate scores, identify weaknesses, and recommend actions to improve every aspect of life.

The goal is that when I open Vinay AI OS every morning, I immediately know:

- How am I doing overall?
- Which area needs attention?
- What should I do today?
- How close am I to my long-term goals?

The dashboard should feel like a personal CEO dashboard for my life.
